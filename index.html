MEF Programs

Minmahaw Education Foundation (MEF) is a non-profit higher educational organization, located on the Thai-Burma Border. At MEF, we believe knowledge is power and eduction is abasic human right. MEF provides not only education but also social security, including food, shelter, security, health care and School supplies for its students. MEF has two operations: Minmahaw Higher Education Program (MHEP) and Minmahaw School(MS).

MHEP program

Minmahaw Higher Education Program is a one year intensive program which prepares students to obtain General Education Development(GED), an international high school equivalency certificate and focuses on giving the essential skills to study at international universities. Students who did not get accreditation from the government and are not able to continue theri further education on theri own are our main priority.

MS program

Minmahaw School has expanded to meet the current needs of young men and women from migrant learning centers and refugee camps by adding a secon year of study, with a vocational focus in Teacher Training. Every students's first year is spent learning academic high school subjects, all taught in English While living in dormitoryies within a block of the school. During that year, they are exposed to various vocational options such as teacher training, hospotality training, computer training and small business organization.
